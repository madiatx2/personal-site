Strategy Philosophy

How I
Drive Results

Every revenue problem has a diagnosis. Every leadership challenge has a structural answer. These are the principles I operate by.

  • 01
    Diagnose before prescribing — revenue programs underperform for specific, fixable reasons. The work begins with understanding root cause, not applying generic solutions.
  • 02
    Make the leadership call — accountability converges where marketing and sales meet. Someone has to own the hard answer. I do.
  • 03
    Drive adoption through governance design, not mandates — lasting change happens when people understand the system, not when they're forced to comply with it.
  • 04
    Build for compounding — short-term wins that don't build infrastructure are just noise. I design programs that grow stronger with each quarter.
